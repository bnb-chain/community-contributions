
# BNB Chain Hackathon Wishlist 2025 H2

This document summarizes key focus areas for developers participating in a hackathon, viewed from the BNB Chain ecosystem perspective. The goal is to provide high-level guidance and highlight project types that align with the platform's strategic interests and community needs.

---

## I. Ecosystem Expansion & Utility

Projects should aim to enhance the BNB Chain ecosystem, focusing on real-world utility and user adoption.

### Key Areas & Focus Points

#### 1. Decentralized Finance (DeFi) 2.0 with AI Capacity

- **DeFi & AI Integration**
	- Build sophisticated DeFi protocols, such as:
		- Innovative lending and borrowing platforms
		- Yield aggregation solutions
		- Structured financial products
	- Ideally integrate with AI capabilities, particularly for automation in areas like curator strategy review and portfolio management

#### 2. Privacy Protection for BNB Chain

- Enable users to make private transactions without compromising decentralization or permissionlessness. Key considerations:
	- **Affordability:** Leverage the low cost of BNB Chain to make privacy protection more accessible
	- **User Experience:** Provide a better user experience and native integration with existing wallets
	- **Compliance:** Address compliance requirements, such as including a proof of innocence mechanism

#### 3. Decentralized AI

- Focus on AI innovations that enhance AI adoption and integrate with the decentralized AI ecosystem, including (but not limited to):
	- Decentralized AI reputation systems
	- Registration mechanisms
	- AI payments
	- Related infrastructure

#### 4. New Frontiers

- Innovative projects focusing on governance and cutting-edge concepts like:
	- Futarchy
	- Neobanks
	- Payments
    - Others

#### 5. Web3 Developer Toolkit

- Create public goods that simplify the developer experience for common use cases. Examples include (but are not limited to):
	- Payment solutions
	- Payroll
	- Merchant receivable finance
