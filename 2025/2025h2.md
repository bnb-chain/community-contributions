# BNB Chain Hackathon Wishlist — 2025 H2

This document summarizes key focus areas for developers participating in the hackathon from the perspective of the **BNB Chain ecosystem**. The goal is to provide high-level guidance and highlight project types that align with strategic priorities and community needs.

---

# I. Ecosystem Expansion & Utility

Projects should aim to enhance the BNB Chain ecosystem, with an emphasis on **real-world utility** and **user adoption**.

## Focus Areas

| **Key Area** | **Focus Points** |
|--------------|------------------|
| **Decentralized Finance (DeFi) 2.0 with AI Capacity** | 1. Innovative lending & borrowing protocols<br>2. Yield aggregation systems<br>3. Web2-like UX that hides crypto complexity<br>4. Structured financial products<br>5. AI integration for:<br> • portfolio automation<br> • strategy review<br> • curator systems |
| **Privacy Protection for BNB Chain** | 1. Private transactions without sacrificing decentralization<br>2. **Affordability:** Leverage low BNB Chain costs<br>3. **UX:** Native wallet integration & smooth flows<br>4. **Compliance:** Proof-of-innocence mechanisms |
| **Decentralized AI** | 1. Decentralized AI infrastructure & standards<br>2. AI reputation and registration systems<br>3. AI-native payment solutions<br>4. Safe autonomous trading agents (TEE, secure vaults)<br>5. Agents should access external data (scraping, feeds) |
| **Prediction Markets & Trading Infrastructure** | 1. Binary, categorical, and continuous prediction protocols<br>2. Retail-friendly UX for speculation<br>3. AMM-based liquidity and payout logic |
| **Launchpads & Token Discovery** | 1. Fair, gamified, anti-bot token distribution<br>2. Governance tools: voting, quests, participation tiers<br>3. Social-driven token discovery & ranking<br>4. Allocation via leaderboards, voting escrow, merit models |
| **New Frontiers** | 1. Governance innovation<br>2. Futarchy<br>3. Web3 neobank concepts<br>4. Payments infrastructure |
| **Web3 Developer Tooling** | 1. Public goods simplifying dev workflows<br>2. Tools for payments, payroll, receivables financing<br>3. Common-use developer utilities |
