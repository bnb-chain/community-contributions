# **BNB Chain Hackathon Wishlist – 2025 H2**

This document summarizes key focus areas for developers participating in the hackathon, viewed from the BNB Chain ecosystem perspective. The goal is to provide high-level guidance and highlight project types that align with the platform's strategic interests and community needs.

## **I. Ecosystem Expansion & Utility**

Projects should aim to enhance the BNB Chain ecosystem, focusing on real-world utility and user adoption.

### **Decentralized Finance (DeFi) 2.0 with AI Capacity**

**Focus Points**

#### **DeFi & AI Integration**

Focus on building sophisticated DeFi protocols, such as:

* Innovative lending and borrowing platforms
* Yield aggregation solutions
* User-friendly products with Web2-like UX that hide crypto complexity
* Structured financial products
* AI-enhanced automation for strategy review, risk assessment, and portfolio management


### **Privacy Protection for BNB Chain**

Solutions should enable users to make private transactions without compromising decentralization or permissionlessness.

Key considerations:

* **Affordability:** Leverage BNB Chain’s low-cost environment for accessible privacy
* **User Experience:** Deliver superior UX and integrate natively with existing wallets
* **Compliance:** Include mechanisms such as *proof of innocence*

### **Decentralized AI**

Projects should focus on AI innovations that enhance adoption across the decentralized AI ecosystem. Relevant areas include:

* Reputation systems
* Registration frameworks
* AI payments and incentives
* Infrastructure for decentralized AI operations
* Safe, autonomous, or proactive **AI trading agents** with proper vaulting, TEE, and online data access (not just model wrappers)

### **Prediction Markets & Trading Infrastructure**

**Focus Points**

* On-chain prediction protocols (binary, categorical, continuous)
* UX improvements for retail speculation
* Automated market-making logic and payout mechanics


### **Launchpads & Token Discovery**

Projects should prioritize innovation in the following spaces:

#### **Fair Token Distribution & Launch Mechanics**

* Gamified launch approaches
* Transparent tokenomics
* Anti-bot minting mechanisms

#### **Community Engagement & Governance**

* Voting systems
* Participation ladders
* Quest and engagement mechanisms

#### **Discovery & Allocation**

* Social-driven discovery and ranking systems
* Leaderboards and reputation systems
* Voting escrow (ve-model) and merit-based allocation


### **New Frontiers**

We are looking for innovative concepts in:

* Governance frameworks
* Futarchy
* Neobanking
* Payments and financial rails


### **Web3 Developer Toolkit**

Projects should build **public goods** that simplify developer experiences for common use cases, such as:

* Payment solutions
* Payroll tools
* Merchant receivable financing
* Other on-chain business or DevEx utilities
